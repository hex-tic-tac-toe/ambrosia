# Infinite hexagonal tic-tac-toe
This is a two-player game that takes place on an infinite hexagonal grid.

## Gameplay
1. Player 1 starts with 1 hex at the center.
2. Player 2 replies with 2 hexes.
3. After that, every turn is 2 hexes.
4. The first player to connect six hexagons on one axis wins.

## Win condition
Connect 6 of your own hexes in one straight line on any of the 3 board axes.
Horizontal and both diagonal directions count.

## Legal placements
Place only on empty hexes.
A new hex can be placed at most 8 cells apart from any other hex.
The board is infinite, so play can expand in any direction.
